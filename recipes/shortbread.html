Shortbread

Description

An incredibly easy, 3 ingredient shortbread recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the butter and sugar and beat until smooth
  2. Add flour and mix until dough is formed
  3. Press dough into greased shortbread tin, and bake at 350 for 30 minutes
  4. Remove and cool completely before slicing
