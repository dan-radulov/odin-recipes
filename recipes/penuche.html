Penuche

Description

Penuche is a fudge-like candy made from brown sugar, butter, and milk, using no flavorings except for vanilla. Penuche often has a tannish color, and is lighter than regular fudge. It is formed by the caramelization of brown sugar; thus, its flavor is said to be reminiscent of caramel.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 cups granulated white sugar
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 Â½ teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and milk in saucepan and bring to boil over medium-high heat, stirring constantly
  2. Reduce heat to medium and continue cooking without stirring
  3. Once mixture reaches soft ball stage, remove from heat
  4. Pour into greased baking dish and let cool
  5. Slice and enjoy!
